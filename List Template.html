Product introduction

Moisture, real feeling. 
Tears are not just moisture, but are formed of three layers of oil layer, water layer, mucin layer. 
The oil layer prevents evaporation of tears, the water layer contains much oxygen and nutrition, and the mucin layer has a role of keeping tears on the surface of the eye . 
"New Nemida Rot Dry Eye" is an effective eye drop for approaching this three-layer structure of tears .
